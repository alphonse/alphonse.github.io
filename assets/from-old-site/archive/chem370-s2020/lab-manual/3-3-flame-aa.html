3.3 Flame AA

Safety

This experiment involves using an e-cigarette for an extended period of time. The cigarette may get very hot over this time period; take care not to burn yourself.

Nicotine is toxic. Wear gloves when working with the vape juice.

Do not breath the e-liquid aerosol or let it into the room â€“ do the vaping step in a fume hood!

Never leave the flame AA unattended when the flame is on.

3.3.1 Vaping

  1. Fill an e-cigarette about half full with your e-liquid.
  2. Use the laboratory vaping pump and collection tuve to vape the sample into a tared 15-mL centrifuge tube. Vape the sample in 5-second bursts followed by 30-second pauses to mimic an actual user. Do this step in the fume hood!
  3. Obtain the mass of the liquid in the tube.
  4. Fill the tube to 10 g with ultrapure water.
  5. Filter the sample into a clean centrifuge tube using a 0.45 \(\mu\)m syringe filter.
